# Privacy Policy for Bike Fixup

**Last updated:** 2026 Marck 13

Bike Fixup ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App") and related services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account information:** Name, email address, phone number when you register or log in (including via OTP/OTPLess).
- **Profile and booking data:** Addresses, bike details, service preferences, and other information you enter for bookings and profile.
- **Payment information:** Payment is processed by Razorpay. We do not store your full card or bank details on our servers; Razorpay’s handling of payment data is governed by their privacy policy.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system, unique identifiers, and app version (e.g. for stability and support).
- **Usage data:** How you use the App (e.g. features used, screens viewed) to improve the service and fix issues.
- **Location data:** Approximate or precise location when you grant permission, to show nearby services, mechanics, or delivery-related features.
- **Crash and performance data:** Crash reports and diagnostic data to improve app stability (e.g. via Firebase Crashlytics).

### 1.3 Photos and Media
- If you choose to upload photos (e.g. of your bike or documents), we use them only for the purpose you provide them (e.g. service request, verification). We do not continuously access your photo or video library; access is limited to what you select in the app.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Create and manage your account and authenticate you.
- Process bookings, payments, and service requests.
- Show relevant content (e.g. services, mechanics, offers) and use your location where you have allowed it.
- Send you notifications about your bookings, orders, and important app updates (e.g. push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging).
- Improve the App (analytics, crash reports, and remote configuration).
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on secure servers and may be processed in regions where our or our providers’ systems operate.
- We use industry-standard measures to protect your data (e.g. encryption, access controls). Payment data is handled by Razorpay under their security standards.
- We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed for legal, dispute, or safety reasons.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Delete** your data or request account deletion.
- **Object or restrict** certain processing (e.g. marketing, non-essential analytics).
- **Data portability** (receive your data in a portable format where applicable).

You can:
- Withdraw consent for location, notifications, or optional features in your device or app settings.
- Contact us (see below) to exercise your rights or ask questions.

For users in the **European Economic Area (EEA)** or **UK**, we process your data on the bases of consent, contract performance, and legitimate interests, as permitted by law.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or higher if required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us so we can delete it.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the App or by other reasonable means. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when this policy was last revised.
